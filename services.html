Church App: Engage Your Congregation

You Bring the Brand—We’ve Got the Tech

We’ve got all the features you need to grow participation at your church with a custom church app—the only thing left to do is customize it to your church’s unique look and feel.

From color, icon, and messaging to different modules, the mobile app studio has everything you need to create your church’s new engagement center, without the hassle of hiring coders or designers.
